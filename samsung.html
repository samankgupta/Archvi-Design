PROJECT NAME: Software Centre for Samsung.
PROJECT LOCATION: Logix Cyber Park, Noida.
PROJECT COST: 6.75 Crores.
PROJECT SIZE: 45,000 Sq.Ft.

Samsung is a South Korean multinational conglomerate headquartered in Samsung Town, Seoul. It comprises numerous affiliated businesses; most of them united under the Samsung brand, and is the largest South Korean chaebol. Samsung was founded by Lee Byung-Chul in 1938 as a trading company.

Samsung R&D Institute, Delhi (SRI - Delhi) earlier known as Samsung India Software Centre was set up as an 11th Software R&D Centre for Samsung Electronics located in Noida. It was established in October 2002.
