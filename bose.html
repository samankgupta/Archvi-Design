PROJECT NAME: Bose Stores in Various Places of India.
PROJECT LOCATIONS: New Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, Bangalore, Pune, Ludhiana, Chandigarh, Jaipur, Noida & Gurgaon.
PROJECT COST: 3.00 Millions to 5.00 Millions
PROJECT SIZE: 600 Sq.Ft. to 2500 Sq.Ft.

Bose Corporation is a company which predominantly makes commercial speakers and home audio products. It is based in Framingham, Massachusetts in the United States. Bose Corporation was founded by Dr. Amar Bose, a sound engineer.

Bose India wanted to establish company-owned many stores with their service centers in all over India to show & to experience their home audio products. Archvi Design got this opportunity and helped the Bose to establish more than 40 company stores with some zonal service centers in the pan India.
