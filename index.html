The ARCHVI DESIGN is a multidisciplinary design office offering services encompassing all aspects of space planning, interior design and project management. Archvi Design Group was formally formed by Mr. Vikas Gupta in 2007. As a Founder-Director and a Principal Architect of the Archvi Design, he has incorporated his planning skills, acute business sense and technological - aesthetically expertise to redefine the working atmosphere in the field of Architecture & interior. The main focus is to manage the design and implementation process through the active involvement of experienced senior personnel supported by computer based systems for programming facility requirements, cost & time control. CAD documentation and asset management. Archvi Design takes every project as an opportunity to establish a long-term relationship with the clients and try to achieve their functional and budgetary goals with the aesthetic sense. The company has successfully completed more than 150 Projects in last 12 years.
