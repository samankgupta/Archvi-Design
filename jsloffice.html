PROJECT NAME: R & D Centre for Jindal Steel Limited.
PROJECT LOCATION: Rohad, Haryana.
PROJECT COST: 7.5 Millions.
PROJECT SIZE: 3500 Sq.Ft.

JSL Lifestyle Limited, another subsidiary of Jindal Stainless (Hisar) Limited, headed by Ms. Deepikaa Jindal is the company that creates spaces that speak of you using stainless Steel as the core ingredient, be it the modular kitchen, in kitchenware, in-home and footprint office accessories to cooking utensils. Different brands under the company’s umbrella are the Arttdinox, Stainless Steel Modular Kitchen & Krome.

R & D department of the same Rohad factory (Arttdinox factory campus) wanted to re-furbish their own office, for which Archvi Design has submitted the conceptual designs and proposal to the concerned authority. This office is in the pipeline and is yet to start.
